TASK 16 – TIME SERIES FORECASTING

Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset

Steps Performed:
1. Converted mixed date formats into datetime.
2. Aggregated sales monthly.
3. Observed clear yearly seasonality and upward trend.
4. Split dataset into train and test (last 6 months).
5. Applied Holt-Winters Exponential Smoothing.
6. Forecasted next 6 months sales.

Model Performance:
MAE: 3534.00
MAPE: 16.67 %

Conclusion:
The model captures overall trend and seasonality.
Forecast accuracy is acceptable for business planning.
